Fifthweek empowers creators to give more to their audience.

How it all started

The reason we looked to build Fifthweek was out of our own need. Not as content creators, but as everyday Internet users. We wondered, in a world where great content is typically given away for free, how much more of the best stuff would be possible if we, as hungry consumers, could pay a handful of pennies to receive it.
