I'm a creator living in the EU, will VAT MOSS affect me?

Nope! You leave that to us.

You will be transacting with us in a business-to-business relationship, as a supplier to Fifthweek. That means we will be collecting payments from subscribers (and therefore handling VATMOSS), which means we can simply pay you as a supplier. Suppliers in business-to-business trade do not get hit with VATMOSS.

How the payment process works

  1. Prices are advertised to subscribers as GROSS. This means everyone sees the same price, and every subscriber pays the same amount.
  2. We will pay the correct VAT for that subscriber, meaning what's left over will change from country-to-country. This is the NET amount.
  3. We will take our 20% fee from the NET amount, and pay you the remaining 80% as a supplier.
  4. If you are VAT registered, we will pay you VAT ontop of that 80%.

An example with a Swedish subscriber for a UK creator

  1. You create a blog and set a GROSS price of $1.25.
  2. James from Sweden, like everyone else in the world, will pay $1.25 for your blog.
  3. We will pay the correct Swedish VAT of 25% out of that $1.25.
  4. $1.00 is left after paying VAT.
  5. We take $0.20 and will pay you $0.80
  6. *If you are VAT registered*, and let's assume in the UK, we will pay you 20% ontop. That's $0.96.
