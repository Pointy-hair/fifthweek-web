Subscribers

On Fifthweek you subscribe to individual creators, and each creator is free to set their own weekly subscription price.

We have no minimum subscription price, allowing everyone to find an appropriate value for their particular content.

Creators

You get 80% of subscription revenue, with no hidden fees.

We have no setup fees and no monthly fees, so you can get started right away for free.

Once you have paying subscribers you get 80% of your revenue, and we use the remainder to cover transaction costs, currency conversion fees and our running costs.

What about transaction fees?

All fees are covered by us.

Who owns the content?

Creators own their own content.

What about VAT?

We handle that for you.

How do I get paid?

We currently support PayPal for paying out, with Payoneer coming soon.
