Are there any setup fees?

Nope. No setup fees or hidden costs. It's free to create a Fifthweek blog. It's also free to share your blog with others. We only take payment when you do.

What about transaction fees?

We pay all transaction costs. There are no additional charges beyond the 20% we receive from any payments you take.

What about tax?

We handle sales tax. We also take care of VATMOSS for those in the EU. We pay you directly for your content, which makes us your only customer. It keeps things nice and simple for you.

Who owns the content?

Creators own their content. Fifthweek does not claim any ownership rights to creators’ content. We are not a publisher, but a self-publishing platform.

How often will I get paid?

We pay creators monthly for their sales generated in the previous month.

How will I be paid?

We support free direct deposits to your bank account in 31 countries and also support international Wire Transfers to every other country, or transfers to your PayPal account. International Wire Transfers outside of these 31 countries will be charged at $15 per transfer.
