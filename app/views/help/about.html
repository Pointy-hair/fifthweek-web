About Fifthweek

Fifthweek is a platform for premium blogs: it's the simplest possible way to make a blog and share it with paying subscribers.

We wanted to create somewhere that lifts all the inhibitions around paying for content - because when you pay, you receive so much more value than you put in. Everyone should get the opportunity to experience great independent publications, lovingly crafted blogs and inside secrets from their favourite industry experts. However, no-one enjoys cycling through bookmarks or having to manage 5 different subscriptions.

We are bringing the discoverability and convenience of the free web to premium content. We are reducing sign-up friction to a single click. We are unifying all of a user's subscriptions into a single feed and making new and relevant subscriptions easy to find. There's no adverts, just great content in its purest form.

Founders James Thurley and Lawrence Wagerfield began Fifthweek in September 2014. The company is based in the UK. Say hello - we love to talk!
